# Wraps
- 1 package of wrappers

# Filling
- 1 lb Ground Pork
- 1 lb Ground Turkey
- Half Napa Cabage
- 1 Bunch Green Onion Chopped
- 1 egg
- 1/2 t garlic powder
- 1 t white pepper
- 1 t ginger
- 2 T. sesame oil
- 2 T. soy sauce
- 3 T. oyster sauce

# Mixing
Cut cabage.
Salt and partially dry cabage.
Mix everything into large bowl.

# Wrapping
Take small spoonful of filling and place in a wrapper.
Fold wrapper around filling.
