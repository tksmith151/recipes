--- Batter Ingredients ---
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup flour (80 g) 50-50 white/wheat
- 1/2 cup milk (150 g)
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 Tbsp sugar (20 g)
- ??? nutmeg
- ??? cinnamon
- 1/2 Tbsp vanilla (8 g)
--- Pan ---
- 3 Tbsp butter

1. Preheat Oven to 425 F
2. Combine Batter Ingredients in Mixing Bowl
3. Melt Butter in 10in Cast Iron Skillet on Stove
4. Pour Batter into Cast Iron Skillet
5. Bake for 20 minutes
