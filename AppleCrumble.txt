3-4 granny smith apples, peeled and sliced
For Crumble Topping:
1/3C flour
1/2C Brown Sugar
1C Oatmeal
1/2tsp salt
1tsp cinnamon
1/4C melted butter

preheat oven to 350 F
Put apples in shallow 8inch pan. Combine all ingredients for crumble and sprinkle over the top
Bake for 30min or until apples are softened.
Serve with vanilla ice cream!
